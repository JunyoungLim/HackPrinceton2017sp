  • Try it out!

The Process

Collecting Data

We used the Stock Market API to graph the trends of the stock prices. We used the news API and Watson to gauge news outlet opinions.

Analyzing Data

With Watson, we are assigning each news headline with a score, which we will correlate with the stock market trend.

Presenting Data

We created a animation for a company you chose to show the relationship with news and stock prices.
