The Process

Collecting Data

We collected the stock market data from 1960 to the present, which consist of 1.7 GB of information related to more than 3,000 companies.

Analyzing Data

Using regression ML algorithms and Ensemble methods, we trained data of the five major companies (Google, IBM, Amazon, HoneyWell, CapitalOne). The training data (from the year of foundation to 2015) was used to train the models, and the models gave the predictions for stock prices from 2016 to 2017.

Presenting Data

We created interactive plots for each company that we analyzed so that people can easily compare the actual stock prices and our own predictions.
